What to Expect in a Financial Advisor

Competence

A good financial advisor should be well-educated, and able to demonstrate a significant amount of experience. CFPs are educated, tested and experienced.

Objectivity

Your needs should be at the heart of all your planner's recommendations. Your financial advisor should consider your situation carefully, then give you advice that best meets your goals.

Integrity

Trust is central to a successful financial planning relationship. You rely on your planner's honesty, professionalism and abilities to achieve your goals.

Clarity

Fair treatment means your CFP will clearly state what financial planning services will be provided and cost. Your planner will also explain the risks associated with their financial recommendations, along with any potential conflicts of interest.

Diligence

A diligent financial advisor will reasonably investigate the products or services they recommend and closely supervise any staff working with you.

Compliance

Your CFP will not provide investment advice or stock brokerage services unless they are properly qualified and licensed to do so, as required by state or federal law.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Hail Insurance Claim process can be a tricky one to navigate, let us guide you through it.

What is the most common area affected by hail?

Although hail has no preference when it comes to the property it can negatively impact, the most common area affected by hail is a structure's roof.

Is all hail damage obvious?

Often times the most costly repairs are the one's you don't see coming. Hail can have a lasting impact on a strucuture's foundation and can lead to leaks, cracks, and overall loss of structural integrity.

Should I hire an independent inspector to survey my roof?

It is highly recommended that you have an independent inspector look at your roof before agreeing to any of the insurance's terms. We recommend it so much that we will send someone to perform an inspection for you for FREE, regardless if you use our services afterwards.

Why are hail claims often times denied?

Structural damage is not the easiest thing to spot, unless your property is being inspected by a trained professional. Often times insurance companies will dismiss claims based solely on the fact that they believe the damage to be merely "cosmetic". Insurance companies will also understate damages in order to keep claims under the deductible amount.
